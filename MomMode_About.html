Just a Mom on a Mission!


Hi, I'm Brittney - a mom, just like you - and I created this app because I get it.

After having a baby, I looked in the mirror and barely recognized myself. I was exhausted, overwhelmed, and felt like my bogy wasn't mine anymore. I loved being a mom, but I missed feeling confident, strong, and like me again.

I wasn't chasing perfection. I just wanted to feel good in my own body - to have more energy, to move with ease, and to look in the mirror and smile again. But I didn't have hours to spend at the gym or time for complicated meal prep.

So I started small.

Simple, nourishing meals. Journaling to stay motivated and mindful. It wasn't easy, but it was real - and it worked. From a size 180.2 lbs. to 163.4 lbs. in less than a month. Almost 20 lbs. down! No skipping meals. No sugarless treats. No working out. The plan was to start working out once I’ve hit 150 lbs. Once I did, the real challenge began. Being able to make time to workout. Whether it be at home or in the gym. I had to make time, out of my busy mom schedule, for me.

That’s why I created this app – not for fitness experts or influencers, but for real moms. Moms who are juggling babies, work, laundry, and everything in between. Moms who just want to feel better, get stronger, eat healthier, and maybe fit back into their favorite jeans.

This isn’t about pressure or perfection – it’s about support, small steps, and showing up for you.

You've got this, Mama!

With love,

Brittney Brady
